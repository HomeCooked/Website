How the Application Works

HomeCooked creates a simple invitation-only marketplace where users can share and exchange home-cooked dishes with the click of a button. By using a smartphone application, HomeCooked connects hungry food lovers with local cooking enthusiasts in their area. The food is cooked in a home or non-commercial environment and made available for pickup at a specified time. In order to provide meals, providers post a photo of the available dish, provide a short headline of the meal, and provide additional information such as estimated preparation time, meal price, and lists ingredients, including the presence of meat and/or allergen information. To order a meal, a consumer enters her location and is redirected to a map of nearby providers. The consumer

selects a meal, and receives a notification with the pickup time and address. Users will be able to rate the experience, including the provider, quality of the meal and the interaction with the user.
